Goal Identification

This step involves complete and detailed understanding of Client's key goals underlying the decision to outsource–like improvement in Customer satisfaction metrics, cost savings, managing a more flexible process/scalability, implementing 24x7x365 coverage, diversification of support resources assuring business continuity requirements,or a mixture of all.The support model is designed to achieve Client's pritized objectives.

Map Existing process

All key support processes of Client are mapped at this stage.Process details(including manual, FAQ etc.)are mapped as online knowledgebase that support training as well as self-help resources.This provides the basis for infrastructure set-up and design of detailed training curriculum for the respective support role.The deatiled document is used to simulate the entire gamut of clients interaction process including escalation and reporting procedure.

Technology Environment

During this stage all technology choices are clearly identified and maintenance process defined. Key processes include, CRM technology environment and related support processes, implementation of security policies and entitlement and reporting interface.

Assessment and Benchmarking

This phase involves a detailed analysis of all the performance metrics. Quantitative and qualitative aspect of customers interaction with the support team during the past 6-12 monts are analyzed to arrive at leading benchmark that would provide the basis for structuring effective Services Level Agreement(SLA's).This exercise fixes both current mandated attainment levels as well as targets to be achieved within pre-determined periods.

Risk Management

During this stage key procedures are designed to exhaustively define and measure risks to performance. consideration the domain of support, this process idenfies the multiple risks to SLA adherence as wel as other dimensions of support delivary that may impact Customers satisfaction adversely. Back-end support processes, including infrastructure are designed manage most risk areas. This stage is key to continue Customer satisfaction management.

Development and Service implementation

The implementation methodolgy concludes with the finalization of an exhaustive Services Implementation Plan (SIP). This documents continues as a living document, providing the fulcum of an ongoing, dynamic outsourced support realtionship between Zealous and clients.
